Cheers, Mo
What is this?

A small weekly project spreading kindness through simple cash donations — helping people who could use a hand.

Who's behind it?

Mo.

How many people are involved?

Just me. It’s a one-person project.

How much are you giving away?

£100 each week to a person or cause that could use it. Not life-changing, but hopefully meaningful.

Are you rich?

Not really — but comfortable enough to share small sums.

What’s the goal?

Honestly? To have fun, help a few people, and maybe spark others to do the same.

Is this some kind of tax dodge?

Definitely not. Sometimes I give through my company to registered charities — which means £100 costs me £81 after tax relief — but that’s just a side benefit, not the point.

Is this legit?

Yes, though I get the skepticism. Feel free to email me if you’d like to verify anything.

Can I nominate someone?

Absolutely — send their story to hi@cheersmo.com.

How do you choose who receives the money?

I look for people who genuinely need it — through social media, community groups, or nominations from friends and strangers.

Is Mo male or female?

No comment.

Can I contact you?

Yes, of course — email hi@cheersmo.com.

Who is Mo?

Nice try, but I’m keeping that one to myself.
